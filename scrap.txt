Thresholds for Diagnosis:

Score 40-50: Severe depression.
Score 30-39: Moderate depression.
Score 20-29: Mild depression.
Score below 20: No signs of depression.

Questions

How often have you felt little interest or pleasure in doing things?

How often have you felt down, depressed, or hopeless?

How often have you felt trouble concentrating on things, such as reading or watching TV?

How often have you felt tired or had little energy?

How often have you had trouble falling or staying asleep, or slept too much?

How often have you felt bad about yourself — or that you are a failure or have let yourself or your family down?

How often have you felt fidgety or restless, like you were unable to sit still?

How often have you found it difficult to relax?

How often have you experienced changes in appetite (either overeating or loss of appetite)?

How often have you had thoughts that you would be better off dead or of hurting yourself?
